## Os Sfamiyos

Or Family. These words are derived from French, but factoring in the Fraponic Alphabet.

pede - Father<br />
mede - Mother<br />
sferede - Brother<br />
sfude - Sister<br />
cusfijere - Male Cousin<br />
cusfijera - Female Cousin<br />
cusfijeres - Both Cousins<br />
tanite - Aunt<br />
oji - Uncle<br />
